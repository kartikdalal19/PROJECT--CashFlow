Features

Here are some of the ways Cashflow can help you manage your money better.

A Budget feature helps users track the amount of money they have available to spend. This feature usually involves setting an initial budget and then updating it as transactions are made.

A Transaction feature allows users to record their expenses or income in different categories, such as food, transportation, etc. Transactions should include information like the category, description, amount, and the date it occurred.

A Goal feature allows users to set financial goals, like saving for a vacation, an emergency fund, etc. The feature will help users track how much they've saved towards their goals, and it could show the progress in real-time.

The Credit feature allows users to track any amount of credit they have available or any outstanding credit they owe. This can be useful to monitor how much credit a user has available and how much they’ve spent.

Our web app helps you visualize expenses with interactive graphs, making it easy to track spending patterns, set budgets, and make informed financial decisions at a glance.

Our web app offers an interactive, user-friendly, and responsive experience. With a seamless interface, easy navigation, and adaptability across all devices, managing your finances has never been more convenient.
